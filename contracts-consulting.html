Contracts Consulting

I’ve worked in publishing my entire working life and most of that is in contracts. For the last 12-odd years I’ve been a contracts manager, first at a literary agency and now at an independent publisher. I also currently consult for the odd author and literary agency – both on deals and the paperwork itself. If you have an offer or a contract and want to run it by me for whatever reason (sometimes I’m just asked a specific question by ongoing clients) drop me a line through the contact page and I’ll get back to you A.S.A.P.

I don’t represent authors myself so don’t ask me to submit your work to editors – I have my own agent and believe they add significant value to a career – but can draft and/or advise on contracts as needed, invoicing by the hour.
