Tom Lloyd - Fantasy Author

Tom Lloyd is the author of the Twilight Reign series of novels (The Stormcaller, The Twilight Herald, The Grave Thief, The Ragged Man, The Dusk Watchman) and its companion collection of short stories, The God Tattoo; the Empire of a Hundred Houses novels (Moon’s Artifice and Old Man’s Ghosts); and his latest series, The God Fragments (Stranger of Tempest, Honour Under Moonlight, Princess of Blood, The Man With One Name, Knight of Stars, God of Night). All subsidiary rights in the Twilight Reign and God Fragments are handled by the Orion Publishing Group.

He is also the author of the stand-alone novella Fear the Reaper, published by Kristell Ink, the self-published SF novel Falling Dark and the historical fiction novel Verona In Autumn, published by SRL Publishing.

Devil Inside - the first in a new series entitled The Devil Diaries - will be coming spring/summer 2025.
